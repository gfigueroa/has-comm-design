I. Course Summary

Sociology focuses on the importance of sociology in understanding cultural influences and socialisation and the role they play in the development of individual personality. Particular attention is paid to such topics as culture, socialisation, social structure and social institutions. This course investigates these topics and their roles in shaping our society. Current events are stressed making connections with topics learned in class and how they relate to current issues in our society. By the end of the school year, we were expected to know: how culture and heredity affect social behavior, the role socialization plays in human development, the five types of social interaction, the importance of social control, and distinguish between formal and informal methods of social control.
