I. Course Summary

In this course, we learnt about history of the United States. Materials we had covered range from the New Spain to industrialization. Famous historical figures we had discussed include Abraham Lincoln, George Washington, Theodore Roosevelt, Franklin Delano Roosevelt, and Woodrow Wilson. Famous historical events we had been lectured on include the American Revolution, the American Civil War, and foreign immigrations. In addition to reading our textbook and listening to in-class lectures, we did multiple powerpoint presentations. As a group, we completed those on Alexander Graham Bell and Jamestown. Individually, I aced that on Sarah Bagley.
