I. Course Summary

In this course, the main fields covered include kinematics, waves and sounds, as well as electromagnetism. Kinematics consists of Newton's laws of motion, and projectile motion. We did a lot of lab experiments, such as slingshot and egg drop. Around February of 2016, we started to prepare for the AP exam and the amount of practice questions was intense.
