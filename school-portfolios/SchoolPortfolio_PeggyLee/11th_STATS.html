I. Course Summary

In this course, we would be learning about frequency distributions, frequency histograms, and normal distributions. We learnt about calculating probabilities. A classic poker problem would be "what is the probability for someone to get a flush out of a deck of card". We also learnt about cumulative distribution function (CDF) and probability density function (PDF). They can be used with any distribution, such as normcdf(x) and normpdf(x).
