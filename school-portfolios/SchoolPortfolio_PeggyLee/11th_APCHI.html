I. Course Summary

In this couse, we do listening exercises on Mondays, speaking practices on Tuesdays, reading comprehensions on Wednesdays, writing exercises on Thursday, and Chinese culture stories on Fridays. We did various projects and activities, which would allow us to explore Chinese cultural, festivals, art works, and calligraphy. By the end of the school year, we were expected to master the following: communicate through listening, speaking, reading, and writing, make comparisons of the languages, cultural concepts, as well as cultural perspectives, practices, and products, and apply skills learnted in class to real-world situations and to become life-long learners.
