I. Course Summary

In this course, we learnt about the basics of theatre, including acting with and without a prop and coming up with our own script. All students participated in the Art and Theatre Night. In groups of three to six, we created our own props and acted out the plays we wrote. Although there were a lot of conflicts in the preparation process, we managed to make it a success.
