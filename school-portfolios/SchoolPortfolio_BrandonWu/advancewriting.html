Course description: In the Advanced Writing Course, you will write different types of essays and expand various writing skills including error correction, grammar usage, punctuation usage, style, summarizing, and paraphrasing. The course will help you to improve your ability to write different types of essays and fine tune your writing skills. A variety of writing concepts and techniques are illustrated to guide you in developing clearer, more organized writing. Also, you will have the chance to practice effective revision of your work with the guidance of your tutor.
