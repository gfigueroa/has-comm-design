        In this course, students will write using proper grammar, sentence and paragraph structure, language and spelling. Students will learn the proper usage of the English language and be able to use it in their everyday writing. Students will learn to use topic, supporting, and concluding sentences as well as appropriate transitions in a 5 paragraph essay format. Students will use the steps of the Writing Process to write personal narratives, expository writing, and persuasive writing assignments. In addition, students will learn to self-evaluate as well as evaluate their peers based on the 6+1 traits of writing.
