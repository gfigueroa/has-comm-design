    Mrs.Kruger
  • Nationality: American
  • Bachelor of Fine Arts: Art and Design – Columbia College Chicago
  • Masters of Education: Curriculum and Instruction – Montana State University
  • Mrs.Kruger is the new Vice Principal at Hsinchu American School this year. She continues to instruct our AP Studio Art, Drawing + Painting, and Middle School art courses. Mrs. Kruger will also serve as advisor for the Fashion Club and co-advisor for the 2016-17 Student Council.
        The emphasis of AP Studio Art is to create an extensive body of work that demonstrates the student’s mastery of artistic concepts and technical skill, their ability to focus their work on a particular visual idea, and their ability to work with myriad mediums and approaches to achieve their visual ideas. Students will produce a minimum of 24 works that satisfy the requirements of the Quality, Concentration, and Breadth sections of the AP Studio Art 2-D Design Portfolio.
Prerequisite Courses/Achievements: Drawing and Painting, or 2-D/3-D Design; Portfolio Review
