  • Mr. Witte will be teaching World Geography, 8th grade Civics, Modern World History, Introduction to Writing, and 7th grade World History for the 2016-2017 academic year. He is one of the 11th grade advisors and co-coordinator of Student Government with Mrs. Kruger.
        This high school level history course will focus mainly on European/Western influence on world history from the time of the Renaissance (14th century) through modern times. It is taught as an overview course of the major events of European/Western history with specific attention given to interactions between peoples and countries (World Wars, Exploration), shifts in ideologies and cultures (Reformation, Enlightenment, Revolutions), and how the events and topic influence each other throughout history.
