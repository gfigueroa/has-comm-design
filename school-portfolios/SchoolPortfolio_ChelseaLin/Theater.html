        The high school theater class is an introductory course in the study of theater. Students will be studying various aspects of theater appreciation including: structure of drama, play production, improvisation, pantomime, acting and technical design, musical production and costume/set design. The course includes scene work in productions ranging from single scene plays to major productions.
