    Mrs.Kruger
  • Nationality: American
  • Bachelor of Fine Arts: Art and Design – Columbia College Chicago
  • Masters of Education: Curriculum and Instruction – Montana State University
  • Mrs.Kruger is the new Vice Principal at Hsinchu American School this year. She continues to instruct our AP Studio Art, Drawing + Painting, and Middle School art courses. Mrs. Kruger will also serve as advisor for the Fashion Club and co-advisor for the 2016-17 Student Council.
        The purpose of this class is to incorporate students newly developed drawing skills into painting. A variety of mediums will be explored, including watercolor, acrylic and oil paint. Upon the completion of each project, every student must participate in a group critique. The ability to engage in the critical analysis of others work, as well as their own, is instrumental to a student’s artistic growth and development.
