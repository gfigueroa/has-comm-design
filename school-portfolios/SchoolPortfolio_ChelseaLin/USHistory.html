        This course will examine the key events, important people, and major movements of United States history from colonial times to the present. Students will be able to compare and contrast major eras and be able to analyze multiple causations. The students will practice note taking skills, reading for analysis, and historical essay writing. The course will build upon concepts and skills learned in elementary U.S. History and middle school World History classes.
