        High School Computer Science has been designed following the guidelines proposed in the Level II Model Curricula for Computer Science published by the Computer Science Teachers Association (CSTA) . It is a 1-year course, divided into two semesters of approximately 20 weeks each. The course is roughly divided into four core components:
• Computer basics
• Computers in society
• Algorithms and problem solving
• Programming
