        This course is the final course in the algebra sequence taking by students at this school. As such, students should have a competent background in Algebra I and Pre-Algebra in order to be successful in this course. Students must be adept at solving equations with one variable, solving systems of equations with more than one variable, manipulating polynomials and different methods of graphing lines and parabolas. Students will hone their skills and take them to new levels with different applications in Algebra II. This class serves as a gateway to higher levels of mathematics such as pre-calculus, AP calculus and sciences such as physics and chemistry will require some of the skills that will be taught in this class. Proficiency in Algebra II will also be necessary to be successful on the SAT and other tests.
