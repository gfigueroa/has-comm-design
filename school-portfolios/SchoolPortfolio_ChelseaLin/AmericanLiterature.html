  • Mrs. Derdiger will be teaching Introduction to Literature, Advanced Writing, American Literature, Honors Novel Study: Dystopian Literature, and co-teaching College Essay. She is the co-advisor for 9th grade and a co-sponsor for World Scholars Cup and Theater Club.
        This course chronicles classic American literature written during the past four hundred years. The course is designed to introduce students to the concept of analyzing literary works within the context of a historical period, and understanding the relationship that literature has with culture and society. These skills will be important to their success in future advanced level Literature courses, as well as their success as a lifetime reader. .
