    Of Love and Other Demons essay
    In the story "Of Love and Other Demons" by Gabriel Qarcía Márquez includes many different types of themes. Among them, the author including the theme of Mixture and Animals vs. Human Races in the story. Auther create lots of differences and similarities that fits into the theme of Mixture and Animals vs. Human Races to show the message of the distances and relationship between humans and animals.Even though human and animal are totally different, but acting in the same way and same feeling or different way and different feeling can create the differences and similarities.
