  • Thanksgiving
    Thanksgiving Day is a national holiday all over the world. As its name implies, Thanksgiving is originally celebrating of giving thanks and bless to the harvest time of the pass year. Thanksgiving celebrate on different country with different date, United States celebrate Thanksgiving on the fourth Thursday of November and Canada celebrate on the second Monday of October. The celebration was very similar all over the world, but though out the world, Thanksgiving still have differences by each countries due to the historical roots in religious and cultural traditions, it has long been celebrated as a secular holiday as well. During November, Hsinchu American School had also celebrate for Thanksgiving. Each student and teacher were encourage to bring food to school and place together like a buffet. After the half day of school the event has finally started, based on the age of student, line by line and pick up the food the want. The atmosphere was lovely and cheerful, everyone were holding a thankful heart to taste the delicious mall. After the Thanksgiving lunch, there was an American football event at our schools soccer field, teacher and high school student were mixed up and separated into two teams, middle school student were playing capture the flag in the basketball court. The event was well planned and funny, every student and teacher are having a wonderful time in this day.
