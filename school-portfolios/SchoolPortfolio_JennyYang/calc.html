COURSE DESCRIPTION
This course is one of the final math courses students will take in high school. It is built strongly on the algebra concepts students have learned and also works with much of trigonometry students have learned up to this point. However, one of the main focus points of this class is to get students to be able to reason and solve problems with different strategies rather than memorizing formula.
