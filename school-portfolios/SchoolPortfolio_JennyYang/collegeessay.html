COURSE DESCRIPTION
College essay will be a comprehensive college application class.The course will cover topics such as the college research process, reasoning for higher education, majors and programs of study, geographic location, costs of living and tuiton, admission requirement and related points. A good portion of the class will be dedicated to the crafting and revising of personal statements and responses that colleges/universities may require as part of the application progess. The second semester of the class will focus on a variety of life skills and topics that students should equip themselves with in order to properly live on their own in a higher education type of setting.
