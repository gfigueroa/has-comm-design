COURSE DESCRIPTION
This course is designed to give students an introduction to basic Spanish grammar and vocabulary. It will also develop students' reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills in Spanish. Students will gain an appreciation and understanding of Hispanic language and culture. Finally, it will prepare students to move on to higher levels of Spanish in high school and beyond.
