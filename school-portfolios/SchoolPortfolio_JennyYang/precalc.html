COURSE DESCRIPTION
This course will mainly build upon mathematical concepts students have learned in Algebra ll and expand upon them. Students will become adept at function analysis for various functions including linear, polynomial, rational, exponential, logarithmic, and trigonometic functions. There is a large focus on trigonometry in this course and students will use various applications of this material.
