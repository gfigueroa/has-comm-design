  • Teacher name: Mrs. Kruger
  • Course description:
    In this course, we explored our artistic abilities by completing artworks of different types. Some of the types of art we’ve done are realistic, abstract, and surrealism. In class, we were mostly given time to work on our artworks after the explanation of the assignment. There isn’t any homework assigned typically besides occasional sketchbook assignments. A very different thing we do in this art class is critiquing. On the due date of every assignment, each student would present their artwork, and other students would critique and comment on it.
  • Examples of major work done in the class:
    All works done for this class has all been either on relatively large paper or 3D material. Thus, no copy of the work is attached and shown on this website. Some works we’ve done are surrealism artwork, facial features artworks, and works that used unusual material.
