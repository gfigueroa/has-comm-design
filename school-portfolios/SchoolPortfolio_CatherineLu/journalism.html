  • Teacher name: Mr. Curtis
  • Course description:
    In this course, students worked on publishing the Literary Journal and the annual Yearbook. Each student would submit layouts and the best layout was ultimately included in the Yearbook and the Journal. The class was lead by Mr. Curtis. At the beginning of each class, students would be assigned a layout. During class, students would each work on their assigned layouts and attempt to complete them by the end of the class. If not completed, the students would finish it as homework.
