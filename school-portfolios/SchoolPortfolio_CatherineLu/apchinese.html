  • Course description:
    In this course, Ms. Wu helped us prepared for the actual AP Chinese test that took place in May. At the start of the year, Ms. Wu came up with a comprehensive plan for the year and throughout the year we followed the plan. Since the actual AP test includes multiple sections (listening, reading, writing, and speaking), each class we would alternate and practice a different section. To enhance our Chinese abilities, Ms. Wu would always assign several worksheets as homework to complete. We’ve also done multiple crafts and DIYs for different holidays.
