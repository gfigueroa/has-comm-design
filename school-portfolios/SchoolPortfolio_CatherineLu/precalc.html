  • Course description:
    In this course, as suggested by the name of the course, we learned the basics of calculus through lectures and examples explained by Mr.Kerr. Examples of topics learned include polynomial division, partial fraction decomposition, and identities. During class, Mr. Kerr would first explain sample questions from the textbook and students will then attempt similar problem types. We were given worksheets related to the concepts every single class and instructed to complete them before the end of class. For homework, Mr. Kerr would usually assigned practice questions from the textbook which vary in complexity.
