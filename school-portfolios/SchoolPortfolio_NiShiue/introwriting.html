That is How You Get Most Improved Student

Have you ever got most improved at something in your life? Like you are horrible at the start, but after that, you do a fantastic job in the end. That's called improvement. This is a story of my first year at Hsinchu American School where I learned that if you work hard, then you can defeat anything.

In the beginning of that school year, I went to HAS, the first day of my middle school. I got problems after one week. My English was as bad as a baby, so teacher suggested me to downgrade to 6th grade. But I answered back strongly to my teacher, "No!"

Suddenly, I began to work hard everyday. I started getting better in the class. I found a tutor to improve my English. If I had problems or questions, my mom taught me how to do homework. My grades like an air plane flew up to the sky. It was unbelievable!

The teacher started to think I was not a student that should be put in the 6th grade. It was amazing for me. When the teacher talked to my mom, I often heard the good things, such as "Ni is very good." or "Look at this score, it is awesome." etc. When teacher asked questions, I often raised my hand to answer. I felt great.

However, in the about the end of school year, it meant the final exams were coming. But I was not frightened, because I studied very hard. My brain only filled by the answers of study guides. I hoped I would be fine on the finals.

In conclusion, my scores of final exams were posted on Canvas. I earned some A's in finals. It's way better than my first semester finals. I felt like a different person. I got some C's in the first semester, but not anymore. I got about three A's on second semester finals. In the last day of the school year, I got most improved student award. So, it's possible to break the door to get to a miracle if you work hard. And that is how you get most improved student in the year.
