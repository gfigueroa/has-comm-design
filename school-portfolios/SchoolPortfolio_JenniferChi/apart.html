In AP studio art, we focus on creating works of art that incorporate the principles and elements of design. In AP studio art, students can choose to focus on drawing, 2-D design, or 3-D design. I have chosen to focus on 3-D design. In class, we are usually given a short lesson on a specific topic that we're talking about like the use of pattern in art or how to experiment with diferent materials. Then, we are given a sketchbook homework and an assignment to complete within the next couple of days. Usually, we get class time to work on our projects. Some of the projects that we have done so far involved creating a piece of clothing using an unusual material and creating a costume design. After we finish a project, we come together as a class and share what we have worked on and earn feedback. We are graded on the quality of our work, our sketchbooks, and our participation.
