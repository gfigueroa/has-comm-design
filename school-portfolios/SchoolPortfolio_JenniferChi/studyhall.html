Advisor: Mrs. Kruger

Study hall isn't a course so much as it is a time for me to catch up on my work. Study hall takes place every A Day and is eighty minutes long. My study hall doesn't have a teacher, but an advisor who watched over us. In study hall, I usually do my homework, extra work from class, or college applications. Sometimes, if I get too tired to do anything, I will take a nap. There are no grades, tests, quizzes, or homework for study hall.
