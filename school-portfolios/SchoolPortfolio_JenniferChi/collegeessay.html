Teachers: Mr. Chu and Mrs. Derdiger

College essay is a class that only seniors cn sign up for. In class, we work on our college applications; this includes essays, recommendation letters, and applying for scholarships. The teachers give everyone "assignments", such as finishing one essay and requesting one application letter, to make sure that everyone is on the write track for application and that no one is procrastinating. Usually, we are given a lot of free time to work on teh things that we need to get down. After everyone has submitted their applications, which will be around the second semester, the teachers will start to teach "life lessons" like how to do taxes or apply for residency.
