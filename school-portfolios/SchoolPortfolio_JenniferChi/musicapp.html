Teacher: Ms. Neagu

This class was an elective class In this class, we discussed different music genres throughout history. We started the year off by learning about the Medival genre and the Classical genre. Afterwards, we began to get into more modern genres such as jazz, rock and roll, and pop. With each genre, we were taught about its historical background, how it influenced certain cultures, and how we felt about the genres. For each class, we usually had to write journals on how we felt about the music that we were listening to. Also, for each class, we usually listened to at least one song from the genre we were currently learning to understand what that music sounded like.
