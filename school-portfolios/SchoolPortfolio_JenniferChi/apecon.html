This course is a college-level economics class. In AP Economics, we learned about the different factors, competition, and markets that make up an economy. For example, we learned about how, in a competitive market, the price of goods and services is pushed by the "invisible hand" and how consumers and producers make up the entirety of a market. In addition, we had many in class discussions about the economy in our current world and the different types of markets that make up our current world. To further our understanding, we were given a multitude of exams and worksheets that asked us questions based on information we had learned in a certain time period.
