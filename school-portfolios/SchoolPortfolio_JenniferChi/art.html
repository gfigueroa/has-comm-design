Teacher: Mrs. Kruger

In this class, we focused on painting and drawing. In class, we created works of art that adhered to an artistic element, principle, or theme. For example, for one of our pieces, we had to focus on painting or drawing two pieces that related to a theme that we came up with. For this project, my theme was "feet", so I created two pieces that related to feet. In addition to projects, we also had written works where we had to write about how we could improve on our pieces. After each completed piece, we had a class discussion about each of our pieces where we had to talk about our idea and help others improve on their ideas.
