Teacher: Mr. Witte

This class was a college-level class. In class, we learned about the cultural, economic, political, and social developments in European history, from the year 1450 to the present, that have shaped todays world. For the class, we usually had to read around a chapter per class to prepare for the material being taught. In class, we took quizzes on recently learned information and held discussions about the information. We also had many AP mock tests where we had to write essays and short answers analyzing and interpreting historical information.
