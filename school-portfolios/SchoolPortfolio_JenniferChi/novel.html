Teacher: Mrs. Derdiger

This class is an advanced dystopian novel class. In class, we read and discuss dystopian novels. So far, we have read "The Hunger Games" by Suzanne Collins, "Anthem" by Ayn Rand, and "Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep" by Phillip K. Dick. For each novel that we read, we learn about certain themes within the novel and we analyze the plot and characters of the novel. In addition, we are alo required to give a certain amount of presentations for each novel where we discuss more themes, plot points, and any information that relates to the novel. We are graded on our reading responses, journals, presentations, and discussions that we have in class.
