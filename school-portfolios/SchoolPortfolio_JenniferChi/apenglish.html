Teacher: Mr. Donnelly

This class was a college-level class. This class focused on English literature and composition. In class, we read a variety of novels and short stories, such as "The Handmaids Tale" and "Norwegian Wood", and discussed the meaning of each piece of literature. We usually discussed the plot of the novels/ short stories and analyzed certain symbols within the novel and what they author was trying to convey. We also had to write a variety of essays to prepare for the AP exam. For homework, we were usually assigned to write a reading response on the novel/ short story.
