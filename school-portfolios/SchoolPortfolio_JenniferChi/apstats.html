Teacher: Mr. Pohlman

This course is a college-level class. While AP statistics is considered a math class, most of the lessons that we are taught are based more on conceptual understanding and interpretation. In class, we discuss how to analyze different distributions of data, how to plan and conduct a study, and how to approah information using probability and statistical inference. The class is very structured, During class, we are given a lecture abou the topic that we are learning. Then, we are given practice problems to do in class and homework to do at home. We are graded on tests, quizzes and homework.
