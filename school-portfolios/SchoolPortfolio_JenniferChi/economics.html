In this class, we were taught the basics of economics. We were taught how to determine the factors associated with the production and distribution of products, how buyers and sellers interact in the marketplace, and the different types of market structures. In economics, we were taught both microeconomics and macroeconomics. Microeconomics is the part of the economy having to do with single factors and the effects of individual decision. Macroeconomics deals with the economy as a whole. In class, we held discussions and listened to lectures. We were graded on projects and tests.
