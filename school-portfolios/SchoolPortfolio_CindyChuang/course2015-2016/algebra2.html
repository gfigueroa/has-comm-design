Algebra II

Mr. Pohlman

In the algebra II course, we learn some math stuff like quadratic function, conic section, vertex form, quadratic formula,
etc. Also, students get to learn about complex numbers and how to add, subtract, and multiply them. Moreover, we learn
many different types of functions such as logarithmic function and exponential function. Basically, algebra II is a class
that continues the algebra-learning after algebra I.
