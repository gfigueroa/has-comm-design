French I

Mr. Chaperon

In the french I course, we learn about some basic french vocabularies and some expressions. Also, we have oral
training and writing tests. For each chapter, we have two writing quizzes, one writing test, and one dialogue (oral) test.
Basically, we are building a fundation for french II course next year.
The following conversation is what I write using some expressions we learn in class:

Professeur: Bonjour! Comment ça va?

Moi: Ça va très bien! Merci, et toi?

Professeur: Bien, merci. Comment tu t’appelles?

Moi: Je m’appelle Charlotte. Et voici mon ami, Pierre.

Pierre: Enchanté!

Professeur: Bonjour, Pierre! Tu as quel âge?

Pierre: J’ai quatorze ans.

Professeur: Bon, je dois aller à la bibliothèque. Au revoir!

Moi: Au revoir!

Pierre: À demain!
