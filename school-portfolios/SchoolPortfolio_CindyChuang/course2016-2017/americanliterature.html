American Literature

Mrs. Derdiger

In the american literature course, we focus on the study and analysis of literature written by authors living in what is
now the United States of America over the last four-hundred years. Moreover, we write responses to texts as well as
essays as we explore the literature being presented. In this course, we also discuss historical events which can help
deepen our understanding of the context.
