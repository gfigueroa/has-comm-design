Week 1

     August 22, 2016 was a really exciting day, especially for the students, because it was the first day of school. The night before the school start, I was very exciting and my mood was exhilarate that my body was full of energy, just like the battery was fully charged, because this summer vacation is just boring, and I can’t wait to meet my friends and new students in school. At the morning, I wake up with no distraction and interference, just like the feeling when I wake up during the summer vacation, no pressure, no bother. When I looked at my phone I just realized that I’m definitely going to be late for school, I packed up my stuff and start heading to school. When I have arrived the school, the ceremony was almost done that I missed the important information about the school. After the ceremony was done, people start moving to their home room. There were lots of new students that has transfer to our school, which create a different feeling to me that I feels like the school was new and fresh to me. All in all, the school has changed a lot, especially the environment has changed a lot, student no longer speaks English in school anymore, and students can no longer use the facilities from the old place.
