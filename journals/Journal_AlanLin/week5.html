Week 5

     From Tuesday Sept. 27th just after midnight to noon Wednesday Sept. 28th, Typhoon MEGI bring very heavy rain and strong winds to all areas of Taiwan, covering the entire island with its storm radius over as it makes its way diagonally across Taiwan. This is a large storm, moving at an average speed. A little more powerful than the last storm that hit Taiwan. Anything over 73 mph / 117 kph is considered hurricane-strength, which we got 2 days of extra holidays.
