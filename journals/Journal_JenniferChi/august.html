Week 1 (8/27/16)

This week, we discussed the qualities that a good website would have. Although, I don't think I really learned anything because most of the qualities we learned about were based off of common sense and opinion. Some of the qualities included were design, navigation and communication of the website. I think the most important thing we discussed this week were the qualities of a bad website. This included whether the website was disorganized, too cluttered, unappealing, etc. I think everyone knows what a "good" website looks like (organized, easy to navigate, well designed), but we don't really consider the qualities of a bad website. We only know that it looks bad. So, I think it was good that we verbally expressed what made a bad website a bad website. However, I think that everyone in the class all had similar ideas of what a good and bad website looked like, so there was no disagreement anywhere.
