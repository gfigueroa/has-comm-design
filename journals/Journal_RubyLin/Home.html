What We learned

University of Washington Online Course

In Unit one
This unit will give you a basic understanding of core design principles that apply to web design and development. It will help you to establish criteria for evaluating the quality of websites, introduce you to color theory and web design standards, and introduce you to the site planning concepts of developing a site plan.

In Unit Two
This unit teaches the basics of HTML coding. In this unit, an emphasis is placed on document content and structure, as opposed to presentation (presentation will be covered in Unit 3). This unit can be completed entirely with a web browser and simple text editor. The product that you create while learning HTML code, and later CSS, is a working portfolio, which you will use to demonstrate mastery of the skills you learn during this course.
