Journal 1 - 08/25/2016

This week, we learned about how to judge whether a website is good or not, and we identified some traits that make a website look well-organized. The thing I related to the most in class was to create a rubric to score a website. I had seen a lot of rubrics that graded many different things such as an essay or project, and by creating a rubric ourselves, we could find out the characteristics that all good websites should have. In addition, I thought the most important point communicated this week was the different features of good and bad websites, and this explained why some websites were well-designed and others not. Lastly, if I could do this week over again, I would communicate more with my teammates and divide the work equally; thus, no one would have to do extra work and everyone could participate.
