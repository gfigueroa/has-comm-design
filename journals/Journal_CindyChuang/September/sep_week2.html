Journal 2 - 09/11/2016

This week, we learned how to group similar information into a bigger category and we had created a school website that contained several categories. The thing that I related to the most was organizing different information. I had seen a lot of websites, some of them grouped information in an unclear way so people couldn't find what they need. However, some of them grouped information in a methodical way that people could find what they need easily. Additionally, we had a group work on Tuesday in which some of my teammates didn't agree with my idea. They believed that "Club" should be classified into the "Student" category, but I thought it should be classified into the "Activity" category. When we disagreed with each other, we explained our thoughts and each one take a turn to give in. I thought the most important point communicated this week was the steps of building a website and switching between different webpages (use <nav>). Lastly, if I could do this week over again, I would change the way we classified information because I felt like there were too many categories which would make our webpages looked messy.
